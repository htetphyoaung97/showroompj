TTAS Co., Ltd. also known as Toyota Aye & Sons is the first Toyota official dealership in Myanmar. Former company, Aye & Sons was established in 1958 by Japanese founder Mr. Jun Hara and his wife Daw Khin Khin Aye. In 1996, Japanese conglomerate, Toyota Tsusho Corporation and U Aye Zaw who is the son of founder of Aye & Sons formed the TTAS Co., Ltd.
